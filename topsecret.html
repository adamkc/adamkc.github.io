▣ Active Deployments

Game · Daily puzzle
Budget Rank

A daily puzzle in the spirit of Wordle: rank five federal policies by how big a dent each makes in the $1.83T deficit. You get square-by-square feedback on every guess, plus a donut showing how much of the deficit your five picks would actually close. New puzzle every day.

Browser tool
Trail Route Editor

Load a DEM in the browser, drag trail vertices around, and let a spring-mass optimizer chase a target grade. Hillshade, contours, 3D terrain, GeoJSON/KML in and out — nothing leaves your machine.

Off-site · Local Python tool
STAG+

A local photo-library tool that auto-tags images and rates them for technical quality (BRISQUE) and aesthetics (NIMA), writing the results to XMP sidecars for darktable. Runs on CPU, no GPU needed. Lives on GitHub, not on this domain.
